Goals

TO PRODUCE COMPETENT GRADUATES WHO ARE IMBUED WITH POSITIVE VALUES AND RICH IDEAS OF FILIPINO LIFE, CULTURE, AND ARE RESPONSIVE TO A SOUND EDUCATIONAL TECHNOLOGY THAT COULD HASTEN THE SOCIAL, ECONOMIC, AND ECOLOGICAL DEVELOPMENT OF THE NATION, ESPECIALLY WESTERN VISAYAS.

Objectives

The program seeks to equip students with the essential knowledge, skills, and attitudes necessary to become effective leaders, managers, and professionals capable of contributing to sustainable economic growth and societal development.
