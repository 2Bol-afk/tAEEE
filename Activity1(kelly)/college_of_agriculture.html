Goals

THE BSA PROGRAM AIMS TO ENHANCE SCIENTIFIC, TECHNOLOGICAL, AND ENTREPRENEURIAL LEARNING THAT IS RELEVANT AND RESPONSIVE TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF GRADUATES WHO WILL BECOME COMPETENT PROFESSIONALS AND LEADERS IN THE FIELD OF AGRICULTURE.

Objectives

The program seeks to provide students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for them to become competent agricultural professionals, capable of contributing to sustainable agricultural development and addressing the needs of local and global communities.
